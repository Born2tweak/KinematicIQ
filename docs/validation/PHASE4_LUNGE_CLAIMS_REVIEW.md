# Phase 4 Forward Lunge claims review

Status: blocked draft; no reviewer signatures and no claim approval.

The evidence chain is blocked at field collection, rater qualification, development selection, freeze, locked count/event validation, synchronized angle validation, and repeat-session reliability. Accordingly, every proposed Forward Lunge metric, finding, camera instruction, confidence statement, and interpretation remains `suppress` for product use. Internal engineering artifacts may use “experimental” and “synthetic fixture” language only with an explicit capture/setup scope and abstention boundary.

Forbidden regardless of future favorable engineering results: FMS scoring; diagnosis or pathology; injury risk; joint/tissue health; forces, loads, torque, power, or muscle activation; normative correctness; readiness or return-to-play; clinical meaning; and availability by implication.

G-CLAIMS requires independent signed reviews from a qualified biomechanics reviewer, claims/product-safety reviewer, and evidence owner, including qualifications, conflicts, line-by-line evidence links, dispositions, and the reviewed-copy checksum. No autonomous test or repository owner approval substitutes for those reviews.
